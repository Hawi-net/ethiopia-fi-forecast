# EDA Key Insights


## Insight 1: Account ownership growth

Evidence:
Account ownership increased between survey years but growth slowed after 2021.


## Insight 2: Mobile money expansion

Evidence:
Mobile money accounts expanded rapidly after digital payment launches.


## Insight 3: Gender gap

Evidence:
Female ownership remains lower than male ownership.


## Insight 4: Infrastructure impact

Evidence:
Mobile network expansion appears associated with increased access.


## Insight 5: Data limitations

Evidence:
Limited yearly observations make forecasting difficult.
